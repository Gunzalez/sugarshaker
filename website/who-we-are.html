Team Shaker

Alasdair Orr Managing Director
Alasdair Orr Managing Director

Al is responsible for all business development, strategy, planning and client services at SugarShaker.

He comes from an agricultural background in north east Scotland and started his career at the Institute of Directors in London.

Moving on to a number of London-based marketing agencies as an outsourced new business consultant before he joined SugarShaker in 2004.

In 2009, he returned to Scotland where he runs the agency on a small farm near Stirling.

A part-time/hobby farmer, he keeps a few pigs, chickens and cattle.

Rob Curle Technical Director
Rob Curle Technical Director

Rob has been leading the development team at SugarShaker since 2008. Prior to that, he ran a couple of small agencies – 4q and twelvenoon.

From a computer science background, Rob cut his teeth in companies such as Sage and Accenture before moving to London in 2000 to see the end of the dotcom boom.

He currently lives on Hove seafront.

Robin Doyle Creative
Robin Doyle Creative

Robin, who has been working in design for more than 15 years, started out designing interactive content for the music industry including a small online offering for David Bowie!

From these auspicious beginnings, he went on to freelance in global agencies such as AKQA and led a range of interactive creative teams.

His work has garned industry awards for his clients, while an interactive kiosk he developed was featured at the Royal Festival Hall and appeared in Creative Review.

He lives in the Devonshire countryside.

SugarShaker Advantages

Advantages over large agencies
  1. Low overheads = super-competitive rates
  2. No middle men = direct contact with the individuals working on your project
  3. More flexible – we only ever pick the right staff for the job so we never shoe-horn idle team skills into clients' projects
  4. All our team members have a vested interest in a project's success
  5. Lower agency retainer requirements
Advantages over freelancers
  1. Many heads are better than one, bringing broader experience of large and small projects
  2. Faster project turnaround
  3. 9-5 support every working day (with no downtime for holidays)
  4. Regular face-to-face meetings and reporting when necessary
  5. Security: all our data is backed up daily on and off-site on our dedicated Rackspace servers, and we're underwritten by Hiscox insurance
  6. Balance of technical v creative v business – few individuals can wear all three hats
  7. Cross-sector business experience allows us to advise on the best ways to apportion budget for maximum return – to increase engagement, increase conversion and sales, and decrease operational costs
